Google Digital Garage

Want to extend your business’s online reach? Considering self-employment? Or think you could enhance your CV by learning about digital skills for business, but not sure how to go about it? We offer a FREE Google Garage digital skills course. Learn how to choose information to include on your website, use search engines to your advantage, compile newsletters your customers will want to read, use social media effectively and more.

Spaces available on Mondays 10am-12noon and Thursdays 2pm-4pm. Spaces are limited, so please book in advance on 01473 263810 or 01473 263827 or at hub@suffolklibraries.co.uk.

Business advisors

We have two New Anglia Growth Hub advisors working on a fortnightly, rotating basis. They advise on topics including exporting, digital support, intellectual property, finance, grants and vouchers and starting up a business.

Kurt Scheepers

Kurt is a business growth adviser for south-east Suffolk and specialises in grant funding and business management.

William Millar

MakerSpace

Situated in the Lab, our MakerSpace is an open, friendly public space to design and create. We offer a range of equipment including two computer coding kits and two professional sewing machines.
