Code club

Learn coding while using your imagination in creative projects. Bring your own laptop or use one of our library Chromebooks (tablets aren't suitable). For children aged 6-14, accompanied by an adult. Call 01473 263827 or email hub@suffolklibraries.co.uk for info.

Business advice

Google Digital Garage

Want to extend your business’s online reach, but not sure how to go about it? We offer a FREE Google Garage digital skills course. Learn how to choose information to include on your website, use search engines to your advantage, compile newsletters your customers will want to read, use social media effectively and more.

This service starts on 31 October, with spaces available on Mondays 10am-12noon and Thursdays 2pm-4pm. Spaces are limited, so please book in advance on 01473 263810 or 01473 263827 or at hub@suffolklibraries.co.uk.

Business advisors

We have two New Anglia Growth Hub advisors working on a fortnightly, rotating basis. They advise on topics including exporting, digital support, intellectual property, finance, grants and vouchers and starting up a business.

Kurt Scheepers

William Millar
