The Library, Books and Reading Team for Schools offers specialist knowledge and experience to support leisure and curriculum reading in schools across all sectors.

Our services include resources, advice and guidance, training and special events. Our 'menu' system provides value, choice and flexibility as you can pick exactly what your school requires.

For many of our services, we offer 'supported' loans, where we provide, select and deliver books to your school, and 'self-service' loans, where your school is issued with a library card you can use at any Suffolk Library. We offer annual rolling contracts and pay-as-you-go services.

Curriculum support

Book and resource loans, guided and group reading sets, artefact loans, KS5 class library cards, dual and foreign language for EAL learners, large print titles.

Training Opportunities

Book support groups, primary staff forums, county training days and secondary librarian meetings.

Advisory librarian support

Our advisory librarians bring over 80 years experience to library design and layout support, computer system set up, skills sessions with students and more.

Awards and events

Book mastermind, book promotion events, author visits and Reading Stars award.

Online resources

Lesson plans and classroom activities, advice on running reading hack groups, and interviews with children's authors for use by teachers.
