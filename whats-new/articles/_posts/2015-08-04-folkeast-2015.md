## [<cite>Andy Cutting</cite> by Andy Cutting](https://suffolk.spydus.co.uk/cgi-bin/spydus.exe/ENQ/OPAC/BIBENQ/5377078?QRY=CTIBIB%3C%20IRN(5523271)&QRYTEXT=Andy%20Cutting)
